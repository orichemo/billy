About Billy

It’s a goat thing... you wouldn’t understand

With being an Anglo-Nubian, or simply Nubian, Billy is known for his large size, long legs, and pendulous lop ears. He already weighs a whopping 145 lbs! But, don’t mistake his sizable stature for being rambunctious. Anglo-Nubians are known to be freindly, curious, and docile animals, which makes them popular pets! It’s no suprise that Billy is popular at birthday parties and Bar/Bat Mitzvahs! Even at the rip age of nine, he’s still the life of the party! Lastly, but most importantly, Anglo-Nubians are also renowned for their tasty milk! Yum!
